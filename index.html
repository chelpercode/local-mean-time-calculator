Daylight Saving Time

„Daylight Saving Time“ (sometimes also known as „Summer Time“) describes the practice of altering the time on the clock by about 1 hour for a certain timeframe during the year.

You probably know this as the practice of turning the clock forward in spring and turning the clock back in autumn.

In case the birthtime lies before 1970, this link might help, though there might be a need to do a little deeper research into the actual situation

Also Note that "war time" should be just another term for Daylight Saving Time. There also seems to be "Double War Time" or "Double Daylight Saving Time" which this calculator does NOT account for.

Longitude of Birthplace

Figure out the longitude of your birthplace here


It is the second value. Please use the decimal value (e.g. "10.39731") and NOT the degree-value (e.g. "10º23'50.12" E").

Also note that the tool above often not just allows you to select the city of birth, but also the rough location within the city (like the specific hospital in the city) which might help in achieving higher accuracy in some cases (although the neccessity of it could be considered doubtworthy).
